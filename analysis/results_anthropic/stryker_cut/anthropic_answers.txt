Question: How long is the distal femoral component?
Answer: According to the surgical protocol document, the dimensions of the distal femoral component are:

Small size:
- M/L (Medial/Lateral): 52mm
- A/P (Anterior/Posterior): 45mm

Standard size:
- M/L: 60mm
- A/P: 54mm

Both sizes also have a fixed valgus angle of 6 degrees built into the design.
